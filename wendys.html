Welcome to Wendy's

About Wendy's

Wendy's was founded in 1969 by Dave Thomas and quickly became known for its square-shaped burgers and delicious Frosty desserts. The chain prides itself on using fresh, never frozen beef in every meal.

Popular Wendy's Menu Items

  • Baconator
  • Spicy Chicken Sandwich
  • Frosty Dessert
  • Wendy’s Chili

Why Choose Wendy's?

Wendy’s focuses on quality ingredients, friendly service, and variety. Whether it’s a classic burger or a baked potato, Wendy’s offers something for everyone. Freshness and flavor make every visit worth it.

Here’s how to make your perfect Wendy’s meal:

  1. Start with your favorite burger or chicken sandwich.
  2. Add a side of fries or chili.
  3. Finish with a chocolate or vanilla Frosty.
